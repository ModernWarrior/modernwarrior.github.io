About

Modern Warrior is a interactive fitness app that allows you to workout with no equipment required and record your progress. Each exercise routine has intervals of exciting martial art movements between each exercise. Keep moving and learn some self defense to maximize your exercise time and get through your workout faster and more effectively. It's over before you know it!
